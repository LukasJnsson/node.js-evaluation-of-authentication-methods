Scenario 3

Passkeys With Username

Information:

In this scenario, you will experience a passwordless authentication system, a modern approach to securing user accounts without the need for traditional passwords. Instead of remembering and entering a password, you will use a passkey in combination with a username. This method leverages a unique digital key, stored securely on your device, to authenticate your identity. By eliminating passwords, these methods reduce the risk of phishing attacks and password theft.

Task:

Enter the provided username into the login form. When prompted, will you will be asked to provide a passkey to be used for login, in this case you will use a phone which is provided by the researchers. Select the phone option when provided with how you would like to set up your passkey. You will then use the phone to login with the passkey.

You can now decide if you want the website to save your information.

Keep in mind that if you do not allow it to save the information you will not be able to recover your password in the future
